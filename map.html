Mission Planner

Here is the place where missions are planned. Click on Current Mission: to see a form for basic mission parameters. On the map you can add markers, polylines and polygons. You can later add more nodes to a struct by clicking on the intersects between two nodes. Each node has a popup to specify target height and AUV speed. These are taken from the default parameters defined under Current Mission:. You can remove nodes by right-clicking,
